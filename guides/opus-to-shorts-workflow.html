Opus.pro to Shorts: My Fast Workflow

Here’s the exact process I use to turn long videos into TikTok, Reels, and YouTube Shorts with Opus.pro—including settings, hooks, and batching.

1) Prep your source

  • Choose video ≥ 5–10 minutes with clear segments and clean audio.
  • Upload captions or enable auto-captions for better hook detection.

2) Key Opus.pro settings

  • Template: Minimal, big captions, bold keywords.
  • Length: 22–32s (sweet spot for retention).
  • Ratio: 9:16, safe margins ON for platform text overlays.
  • Auto cuts: Start with 6–10; promote the top 2–3.

3) Hook formulas that work

  • “You’re doing X wrong—here’s the 10-sec fix.”
  • “Steal this Y system (I use it daily).”
  • “If you’re audience, stop scrolling—try this.”

4) Batch, label, and test

  • Batch 20, publish 1–2 daily. Track CTR and 3-sec hold.
  • Repurpose winners with new hooks and first frames.
